Role & Responsibilities of Ambassadors

Role

  • Information dissemination
  • Act as a point of contact for SkillKalp
  • Organize micro-events in sync with SkillKalp
  • Ensure registrations of peers
  • Create student WhatsApp groups
  • Promote SkillKalp on social media

Responsibilities

  • Event planning & campus promotions
  • Connecting with student clubs/groups
  • Driving SkillKalp initiatives
  • Fostering campus relationships
  • Reporting activities to SkillKalp team
  • Managing campus WhatsApp/social groups

Eligibility

  • Must be a currently enrolled student in an engineering college
  • Strong communication skills and leadership mindset
  • Minimum 3-month commitment
